# Audio Files Setup

## Required Audio Files

### Calm/Peaceful Sounds
Place these files in this directory:
- `calm.mp3` - Peaceful meditation background (forest, rain, gentle music)

Recommended sources:
- https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/meditation/
- https://freesound.org/search/?q=forest+ambience
- https://www.zapsplat.com/sound-effect-category/ambiences/

### Horror Sounds
Place multiple horror sound files:
- `horror1.mp3` - Scream or sudden scare
- `horror2.mp3` - Eerie whisper or distorted voice
- `horror3.mp3` - Creaking or unsettling ambient
- `horror4.mp3` - Static or glitch sound
- `horror5.mp3` - Deep drone or ominous tone

Recommended sources:
- https://freesound.org/search/?q=horror+scream
- https://www.zapsplat.com/sound-effect-category/horror/
- https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/scary/
